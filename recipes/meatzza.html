Meatzza

Delicious, Quick, and Easy!

Carnivore/Keto Meat Based "Pizza"

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Harmons Chicken Italian Sausage
  • 1 8 oz bag Kroger Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Harmons Fresh Mozzerella ball
  • 1 package sliced Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix the sausage, egg and half of the parmesan cheese together in a mixing bowl.
  3. Make it into a crust in a pyrex pie pan or similar deep dish pan.
  4. Bake "crust" at 350 for 20 minutes, and drain/soak as much of the grease off as possible with paper towels.
  5. Slice the mozzerella and place along the crust.
  6. Spread the pepperoni on top of the mozzerella and top it with the remaining parmesan.
  7. Place pan in oven for about 15 minutes.
